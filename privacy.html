Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Ankore ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our voice-based AI wellness companion.

2. Data We Collect

Ankore is designed with privacy at its core:

  • Voice recordings: Processed in real-time for transcription. Audio is never stored on our servers.
  • Conversation text: Stored only on your device and encrypted on the Sui blockchain under your control.
  • Authentication: We use Google OAuth for sign-in. We only receive your name and a unique identifier — never your password.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide AI-generated wellness responses
  • To enable voice-to-text transcription
  • To encrypt and store your conversations on-chain

4. Data Storage & Security

Your conversations are encrypted before being stored:

  • End-to-end encryption using AES-256
  • Stored on the Sui blockchain (decentralized)
  • Only you hold the decryption key
  • We cannot read your conversations

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google: OAuth authentication only
  • Groq: AI language processing (does not retain your data)
  • Deepgram: Speech-to-text (audio not stored)
  • ElevenLabs: Text-to-speech (no data retention)

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Delete your conversations
  • Export your data
  • Disconnect your wallet

7. Children's Privacy

Ankore is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at privacy@ankore.app

Your data. Your control. Always.
